I am an Associate Professor at the China School of Banking and Finance, University of International Business and Economics.

My research interests include finance, international economics, and international business.
 
### Education

- Ph.D. in Finance, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore Management University, Singapore, 2013–2018
- M.S. in Economics, Faculty of Social Sciences, University of Macau, Macao, 2011–2013
- B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University, Beijing, 2005–2009
